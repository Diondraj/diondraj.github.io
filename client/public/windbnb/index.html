End to End Website

Website design and development

My app is a React app that utilizes the MERN stack. Based off of the popular accommodation booking app Airbnb, my app incorporates a unique feature that allows friends to plan together. Using mongoose models and Express for the backend, my website offers a seamless and user-friendly experience for users to book accommodations and plan trips with their friends.

Role: UX/UI Designer, Full-Stack Web developer
Duration: 2 weeks
UX tools: Figma
Frontend tools: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React
Backend tools: MongoDB, Express.js, Nodejs, Passport authentication

Check out website Github repo

Overview

Background

Inspired by Airbnb website, Introducing a new design to a feature that lets you plan your next vacation with friends like never before. With our 'Invite Friends' feature, you can easily collaborate with your closest companions to find the perfect accommodation that meets everyone's needs. Say goodbye to the hassle of planning your trip alone and join.


Problem

It appears that the current design of the Airbnb invite feature is not as visible as it could be. As such, I would like to suggest a new design for the process of inviting and planning a trip with friends on Airbnb.
